Welcome!
Every night before you are going to sleep, think about your day for a moment and write down one thing, which was the most important event of this day.
By the end of the week, you have to select the event that was the most important one.
At the end of the month you should select the most important event of the month, then a quarter, a year...
Relax and analyze events, strive to do more.

How do you think what will be the most important event of the next month?

Start using app right now and try to use it in a long term.

Have fun!

How can the EventShip help you?

Store

Every evening write what was most important during a day. For example: was on movie, closed big project, sex.

Choose

When a week is ended, you should select “the most important event of the week” from the days that were within a week.

Relax

At the end of period (week, month, year) look at stored events and think: "Was the period productive and happy?".

Why EventShip is useful for me?

I made this app for myself. And now I want to share my own story with you. I will show you how the app is helping me.

Productivity analysis
One week was fairly routine at work. At the weekend I was doing various household chores. In this week, the most “important” event was “trip to the market”. I was upset when I discovered it. Couldn’t I do something really important for that week? There were months sometimes when nothing particularly important happened. Awareness of this fact motivates a lot.

Analysis of the importance of events
Sometimes we do not notice what is really important in our life and what is not. One of the good practices is the choice of an important event. In early 2013 I had planned a trip to another city for a conference of developers. I was already sure in advance that this event would be the most important for the week, month, and possibly for the quarter of the year. But just 1 day before the trip, I decided to leave the research degree, I will not delve into the reasons for this decision, but it turned out to be courageous enough and not typical for me. And the very fact that such a decision turned out became more important than the whole trip.

Pictures of the Past
“Let your past go”, “live in the present”. Sometimes, I thought, should I use this technique? It’s really “raking over the dust and ashes of the past.” But as it turned out, it was fun and nice to look at events. As 2013 drew to a close, I saw what I did all year. I had forgotten many of the events and I was pleased to recall them. Thus I began to refer to this technique as a special form of photographs, which are sometimes nice to watch again and reminisce.
